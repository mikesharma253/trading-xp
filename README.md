Trading XP
Live Demo: https://mikesharma253.github.io/trading-xp/
I made this because I wanted to play around with trading without risking actual money or dealing with the nightmare of setting up a real brokerage account. So I just... built one myself instead.
Why I Even Made This
Honestly, I got obsessed with the idea of how people actually behave when they're trading. Like, I'd see someone panic-sell their entire portfolio over nothing, and I was curious if I could recreate that feeling in a low-stakes environment. Plus I wanted to test out some dumb trading ideas without looking stupid or losing my rent money.
What You Can Do With It

Buy and sell assets without worrying about losing real money
Watch your portfolio P&L update in real time as prices move
Test out whatever trading strategy you're thinking about
Use it on desktop or mobile, doesn't really matter

How I Built It
Just vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No React, no Vue, no fancy framework stuff. Wanted to keep it simple so I could actually focus on making the trading logic work and the UI not look completely trash.
The Annoying Part
Getting the charts and price updates to run smooth without everything lagging was actually painful. And I went through like five different layout designs trying to make it feel like a "real" trading app instead of just some janky website.
What I Actually Learned

How to handle state changes in JavaScript without breaking everything
Making UI updates feel responsive and snappy actually matters
Small animation details have way more impact than I thought
Building something that feels real takes way more iteration than expected
